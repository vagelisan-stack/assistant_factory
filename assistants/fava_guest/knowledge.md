Fava’s Villa on the Rocks
Guest House Manual & Local Guide

1) Location & Beach Access
The villa is located directly above Fava Beach, in the University Professors’ residential area of Vourvourou (Sithonia, Chalkidiki). You can reach the beach in just a few minutes via a private footpath.
Fava Beach is a public, unorganized beach (no beach bars, umbrellas, or sunbeds), ideal for swimming. Its natural setting makes it a wonderful spot for families and groups.

2) Quick Distances
Approximate driving distances:

Agios Nikolaos: ~14 km

Neos Marmaras: ~32 km

3) Nearby Towns & What You’ll Find There
Vourvourou village (very close, ~2–3 km):
Small local supermarkets, cafés, ice cream shops, and many dining options (tavernas, pizza, pasta, seafood, burgers).
Nikiti (larger town):
Large supermarkets, pharmacies, ATMs, and a wide selection of shops, restaurants, cafés, and bars.
Don’t miss Old Nikiti (Upper Nikiti), a charming traditional settlement with picturesque lanes.
Agios Nikolaos:
Local Health Center, pharmacies, and dining options mostly gathered around the main square.
Neos Marmaras:
A lively town with entertainment and services including pharmacies, banks, police, and doctors.

4) Boat Trips & Mount Athos Cruises
From Ormos Panagias, you can take a boat cruise around Mount Athos (viewing from the sea) and enjoy the seaside tavernas and cafés.
Tip: Boat schedules can vary by season, so it’s best to confirm locally.

10) Nature Notice
The villa is surrounded by nature. It is normal to encounter mosquitoes, bees, insects, and occasionally other wildlife. The property is regularly treated for pests and rodents, and preventive measures are taken regarding possible snake presence.
The garden is natural and typical of the Chalkidiki landscape, with rocks, pine trees, and olive trees.

11) House Rules (Important)
